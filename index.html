Star Wars RPG

In a galaxy not so far away, there exists an exiting Star Wars Application you should play.

Start by clicking on a character you wish to play with, and then choose your first enemy. The goal is to defeat all the enemies by clicking the 'Attack' button, and bring their HP to 0 before your HP runs out.

Choose wisely because as soon as you attack you will be counter attacked by the enemy! Your 'Attack Power' will grow with each successful attack, but your enemies will remain constant. Good Luck
